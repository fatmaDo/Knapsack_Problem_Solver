Welcome to the Knapsack Solver

The Knapsack Problem is a classic optimization challenge where the goal is to maximize profit while staying within a charge limit.

Why Use This Tool?

  • Flexible: Suitable for logistics, resource management, and more.
  • Easy to Use: Add your items and get results instantly.

How to Use

  1. Set the total capacity of the knapsack.
  2. Enter the weight, profit, and optionally the name of each item.
  3. Choose whether an item must be included by checking "required".
  4. Ensure the same unit for charges and capacity and the same unit for all the profits.
  5. Click "Solve" to see the optimized selection of items.
